The 9-Step Precision Protocol

Step 01

Consultation

Initial strategy session to define your target vehicle parameters, budget thresholds, and importation requirements.

Step 02

Deposit

Activation of sourcing privileges via a fully refundable security deposit, securing your position in our auction bidding queue.

Step 03

Daily Sourcing

Curated selection of daily auction inventory. We filter thousands of listings to present only the highest grade opportunities.

Step 04

Inspection

On-site physical appraisal by our Japanese technicians. Verification of auction sheet accuracy and mechanical integrity.

Step 05

Auction Bidding

Execution of the bidding strategy. Our agents operate in real-time across all major Japanese auction houses.

Step 06

Acquisition

Upon a successful bid, the vehicle is secured and moved to our export yard for final high-resolution documentation.

Step 07

Shipping

Logistics coordination for Ro-Ro or Container shipping. All export documentation is processed with Japanese customs.

Step 08

Customs

Managing arrival logistics, duty payments, and local compliance inspections to ensure legal road registration.

Step 09

Delivery

Final handover at your designated location. The completion of your precision automotive acquisition.
